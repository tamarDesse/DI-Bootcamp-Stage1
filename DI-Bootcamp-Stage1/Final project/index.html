Personal Life:

In 2008, Knowles married rapper and music mogul Jay-Z in a small, private ceremony in New York City.
In addition to acting and performing, Knowles ran a clothing line called House of Dereon with her mother.
She also launched her own fragrance, Heat, in 2010.
Throughout her career, Knowles has served as a spokesperson and model for several other brands, including L'Oreal and Tommy Hilfiger.
Beyoncé miscarried around 2010 or 2011, describing it as "the saddest thing" she had ever endured.
On January 7, 2012, Beyoncé gave birth to a daughter, Blue Ivy, at New York City.
On June 13, 2017 she gave birth her twins a daughter named Rumi and a son named Sir, at California.
